Chutneys

 

Cilantro Chutney

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cilantro leaves washed and chopped (approximately 2 bunches)  
  • 2-4 green chilies(Jalapeno/Serrano) 
  • 2 tablespoon fresh/dried shredded coconut (preferably fresh) 
  • 1 clove garlic(optional) 
  • 2 tablespoon lime juice  
  • 2 teaspoon sugar  
  • 1 teaspoon salt ( as per taste)  
  • ½ cup warm water  

 

Method:

In a blender add all the ingredients to make a smooth paste.  

Add water slowly as needed to keep the blender running. 



Eggplant Chutney

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant: 2 (big)
  • Tamarind paste: 1 table spoon
  • Olive or any vegetable oil: 1 table spoon
  • Chopped green coriander (Cilantro leaves) ½ cup
  • Chopped green peppers: about 5 or more depending upon how much Hot you can endure!
  • Spices: Turmeric powder - ½ teaspoon, Black mustard - 1 teaspoon, Asafoetida powder - ¼ teaspoon
  • Salt - To taste

Method:

Wash the eggplant. Slightly poke the eggplant with a knife. This will help the eggplant not to burst while roasting.Brush oil on the skin. Roast it in the oven 400 degrees for about 10min. When it is done, peel the skin, mix the tamarind paste, salt, chopped green chillies, and mash it well.  In a sauce pan add oil . When it is hot add the black mustard, turmeric powder, and asafoetida. Add the mashe'd eggplant, turn off the heat and mix well. Remove immediately into a serving bowl.  Mix chopped coriander leaves. 



Ginger Chutney

Ingredients:

  • 1 teaspoon chopped ginger
  • ¼ cup grated jagary
  • 2 tablespoons chena dal
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal
  • 5 red chilies
  • ½ teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon tamarind paste
  • A pinch of Asafoetida
  • 1 teaspoon oil

Method:

Fry chena dal, urad dal, mustard seeds, red chilies and asafoetida in 1 tablespoon oil. Fry till the dals become brown and the mustard seed split. Wait till the mixture becomes lukeworm. Then add salt and grind the mixer into a powder. Add chopped ginger, grated jaggery, tamarind paste and water and grind again to a thick paste. Your ginger chutney is ready. 



Gongura Chutney 

Ingredients:

  • Gongura (Roselle) - 1 bunch - about 5 stems. 

  • Red Chilies - 1 cup - 8 to 10 Red chilies. 

  • Garlic - 4 cloves. 

  • Turmeric. 

  • Molasses(brown sugar). 

  • Salt to taste 

  • For seasoning: Oil/Ghee, Mustard, Methi, Urad Dhal and Hing (Asafetida). 

     

Method:

  1. Separate gongura leaves from the stems and wash the leaves throughly.  

  2. Spread the leaves on towel for removing the moisture off the leaves. 

  3. Heat a pan on low heat, fry gongura leaves with very little oil or ghee.  

  4. Remove into a food processor. In the same pan roast the garlic cloves and red chilies. (Optional: If you want extra spicy or want to add green chilies instead of red chilies, Green chilies needs to be dry fried too). 

  5. Add chilies, garlic, salt, turmeric and a small amount of molasses (say a size of peanut) with the gongura leaves and grind into a fine paste. 

  6. Season the chutney with Mustard, Methi, Urad Dhal and Hing (Asafetida) in hot oil or ghee. 

  7. Serve gongura chutney with Rice - topped with Ghee.  



Mint Chutney 

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch fresh mint 
  • 1/2 bunch fresh cilantro 
  • 1/2 inch piece of ginger root 
  • 2 cloves garlic 
  • salt to taste 
  • 1-3 green chilies(Jalapeno/Serrano) 
  • 4 tablespoons lime juice/apple cider vinegar 
  • 2 teaspoons sugar 
  • 1 medium red onion 
  • 1/4-1/2 cup water for blending  

 

Method:

Add all ingredients except the water together in a blender or food processor.  

Blend/process and add water as you blend to make it the consistency of pancake batter. 



Minted Cucumber - Onion 

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces yogurt  
  • 1-2 seedless cucumbers, peeled and diced  
  • 1/4 cup mint, finely chopped  
  • 1/2 medium red onion, finely diced 
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice  
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste 

 

Method:

Place the yogurt in a medium bowl.  

Add the remaining ingredients and season with salt and pepper to taste.  

Cool in refrigerator before serving.

 

 

Peanut Chutney - I

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1 medium sized onion(optional)
  • 4 green chillies
  • 1 small bunch of coriander

  • ½ cup yogurt or water
  • salt to taste


Seasoning:

  • 1 tbsp. oil
  • 1 tsp. mustard seeds
  • a sprig of curry leaves


Method:

Grind to a fine paste all the ingredients, adding enough water or yogurt. Season.

 

Peanut Chutney - II

Ingredients:

  • Peanuts
  • red chilly powder
  • salt
  • a dash of cumin seed powder
  • garlic paste

Method:

Roast peanuts till skin can be removed easily. Powder in mixer / grinder. Do not make the powder very fine. Remove from mixer. Add other ingredients.
