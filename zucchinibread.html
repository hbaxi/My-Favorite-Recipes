Zucchini Bread

 

Wet Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vegetable oil 
  • 2 cups shredded raw zucchini(squeeze excess liquid out) 
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract 
  • 3 eggs 

 Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour 
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder 
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda 
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon 
  • 1 teaspoon salt 
  • 1 ¾ cups sugar 
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts  

 

Method:

Put zucchini in strainer and press or squeeze with hands to get some of the excess liquid out.  

In a mixing bowl beat the eggs, oil and vanilla together.  

Stir in the flour, baking powder, sugar, soda, cinnamon, salt, and walnuts, mixing just until all ingredients are combined. Add drained zucchini. Mix well.  

Pour into 1 large greased and floured bread loaf pan.  

Bake for 60 to 75 minutes at 350 F, or until a wooden pick or cake tester inserted in center comes out with very little or no crumbs clinging to it. 

Set the pan on a wire rack to cool for ten minutes.  

Remove the loaf from the pan and let cool on the wire rack. Serve warm or fully cooled. The loaf can be wrapped in plastic and stored at room temperature for about four or five days.
