Cupcakes

 

Chocolate Cupcake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 ½ cups all purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa 
  • 1 tsp baking soda 
  • ½ tsp salt 
  • ½ cup vegetable oil 

  • 2 tbsp distilled white vinegar 
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract 
  • 1 cup water 

 

Method

Preheat oven to 350F.  

In a large bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients. In a separate bowl, whisk together all wet ingredients  

Pour wet mixture into dry mixture and whisk until combined. 

Divide batter among cupcake cups. Bake until a toothpick inserted into a cupcake comes out clean, 22 minutes. Let cupcakes cool completely in pan.





Coconut Cupcake

  • ½ tsp salt

Frosting 1

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup not fat yogurt 

  • ¼ cup peanut butter

  • ¼ cup cocoa powder

  • 2 cups sugar

  • 3 tablespoons Heavy Cream 

Beat all ingredients together until smooth and fluffy, approximately 3 minutes.
