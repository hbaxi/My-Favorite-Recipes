Biryani Recipes

 

Kachhi Biryani(Oven)

Marinade: 

  • 1 lbs. chicken
  • ½ bunch coriander leaves, roughly chopped
  • ½ bunch mint, roughly chopped
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 1 green chilli (you can use more if you like your food more spicy)
  • ¼ tsp red chilli powder (you can use more if you like your food more spicy)
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp javeri mace
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup fried onions
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 pepper corns
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • ¼ cup yoghurt
  • Salt to taste

Rice: 

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ tsp black cumin
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Layers: 

  • ¾ cup fried onions
  • ½ bunch coriander leaves, roughly chopped
  • ½ bunch mint, roughly chopped
  • 1 pinch saffron, dissolved in ¼ cup warm milk

Method:

Marinade: To make the marinade, mix all the ingredients together in a big bowl, add the chicken pieces to it, mix well and marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours (or overnight).

Rice: When you are ready to cook your biryani, prepare the rice. Wash it and soak it in cool water for 30 minutes. Fill a big pot with water, add salt and oil and bring it to a boil. Cook the rice in it for 4 or 5 minutes. When the rice is ready, drain it and keep it warm separately.

At some point make sure that you PREHEAT the oven to 400 F

Final Preparation:

  • In a big pot, spray with some oil/ghee and add the chicken and its marinade to the bottom. Make sure to spread it all around.
  • Add the mint, coriander leaves and the fried onions.
  • Spread warm drained rice on top of the chicken. Make some holes in the rice and pour the saffron milk through each of the holes.
  • Top up with some more of the fried onions.
  • Put a tight lid on and place covered pan in 400 F oven for 20 minutes. Lower temperature to 300 F and let stay for 20 more minutes.


    • Kacchi Biryani(Pressure Cooker)

    Marinade: 

    • 1 lb Chicken
    • ¼ tsp Turmeric
    • ¼ tsp crushed dry mint
    • 1 ½ teaspoon Salt
    • ¼ tsp crushed cardamom
    • ¼ tsp crushed cloves
    • ¼ tsp cinamon
    • ½ teaspoon garam masala
    • 2 teaspoons ginger+garlic paste
    • 3 teaspoons Lemon juice
    • 1 cup Yogurt
    • ¼ teaspoon chili paste

    Rice: 

    • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
    • 1 tsp salt

    Other ingredients: 

    • Olive Oil
    • 6 tbps fried onions
    • Anise stars
    • 4 bay leaves
    • ¼ tsp Shah Jeera
    • 2 tbps fresh lemon juice
    • a few pinches of saffron

    Garnishing: 

    • Fresh cilantro - 1 handful
    • Fresh mint - 1 handful

    Method:

    Marinade: Mix all marinade ingredients and marinade for 3-4 hours, prefererably overnight.

    Rice: When you are ready to cook your biryani, prepare the rice. Wash it and soak it in cool water for 1 hour. Fill a big pot with water, add salt and oil and bring it to a boil. Cook the rice in it for 4 or 5 minutes. When the rice is ready, drain it and keep it warm separately.

    Final Preparation: In the pressure cooker saute the following:

    • Olive Oil or Ghee
    • few Anise stars
    • 4 bay leaves
    • Marinaded chicken
    After you add the chicken, saute for 5 minutes
    In the pressure cooker stainless steel pan layer the following in order.
    • Marinaded chicken and spices
    • Fried onion
    • Soaked/Partially cooked rice
    • a few pinches of Saffron
    • Fried onion
    • 1 cup of water
    Add more salt, if required, to the rice. Pressure cook, in electric cooker, for 4 minutes.

    Garnish with chopped cilantro and chopped mint before serving.


    Kachhi Biryani(Solar Oven)

    Marinade: 

    • 1 lb. chicken
    • 1 cup yogurt
    • ½ tsp Tumeric
    • ½ tsp crushed dry mint
    • 2 tsp salt
    • ¼ tsp cinnamon
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 2 tsp ginger+garlic paste
    • 2 tsp lemon juice
    • ¼ tsp chili paste

    Rice: 

    • 2 cups basmati rice
    • 1 tsp Salt
    • ½ tsp Shahi Jeera(black cumin)

    Method:

    Marinade: Mix all the marinade ingredients together in a big bowl, add the chicken pieces to it, mix well and marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours (or overnight).

    Rice: Soak 2 cups rice with some water, salt and shahi jeera for an hour. Drain rice.

    Preheat the solar oven

    Final Preparation:

    Layer two pans each with 1 tbsp ghee/olive oil, 3 bay leaves
    Add a small/tiny/spice cloth bag filled with 5 pepper corn, 3 cloves, 3 whole green cardamom pods and a few anise stars
    Next layer, divide up the rice equally into 2 pans
    Next layer, divide up the marinaded chicken equally into 2 pans
    To each pan, add 1 cup water and a pinch of saffron.
    Next layer, add 3 tablespoons of fried onion to each pan.

    Cover each pan with parchment paper+steam tray to form a seal. Add a couple of tiny slits to the parchment paper to help with venting of steam.
    Add to pre-heated grill and leave for 1 hour 15 minutes.

    Chicken Biryani is ready to be served.
    Remove pans and mix things around gently with fork.

    Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro and/or chopped fresh mint before serving.
