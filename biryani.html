Biryani Recipes

 

Kacchi Biryani(Pressure Cooker)

Marinade: 

  • 1 lb Chicken
  • ¼ tsp Turmeric
  • ¼ tsp crushed dry mint
  • 1 ½ teaspoon Salt
  • ¼ tsp crushed cardamom
  • ¼ tsp crushed cloves
  • ¼ tsp cinamon
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 teaspoons ginger+garlic paste
  • 3 teaspoons Lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp Yogurt
  • ¼ teaspoon chili paste

Rice: 

  • 2 Cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 tsp salt

Other ingredients: 

  • Olive Oil
  • 6 tbps fried onions
  • ¼ tsp Shah Jeera
  • 2 tbps fresh lemon juice
  • a few pinches of saffron

Garnishing: 

  • Fresh cilantro - 1 handful
  • Fresh mint - 1 handful

Method:

Marinade: Mix all marinade ingredients and marinate for 3-4 hours, prefererably overnight.

Fried Onion: Thinly slice onion and fry the onion with olive oil or ghee in pan until golden brown. Keep aside for garnishing.

Rice: When you are ready to cook your biryani, soak rice and Shah Jeera in cool water for 20-30 minutes and drain.

Broth: Cook chicken in pan for 10 minutes until all the water from the chicken gets released. Drain and separate the liquid broth from the chicken and keep separately.

Final Preparation:In the pressure cooker stainless steel pan, layer the following in order.

  • 4 bay leaves, 5 anise stars, 5 pepper corn, 3 cloves, 3 whole green cardamom pods
  • Soaked/Drained Rice
  • Marinaded/partially cooked chicken with spices
  • Olive Oil or Ghee
  • Left over chicken broth + 2 Cups of water
  • a few pinches of Saffron
Add more salt, if required, to the rice. Pressure cook, in electric cooker(Instant Pot), for 5 minutes. Turn off pressure cooker and let it sit for 10 minutes, after which do a quick release of pressure and let it sit until ready to serve

Mix/Garnish with fried onion, chopped cilantro and chopped mint before serving.


Kachhi Biryani(Solar Oven)

Marinade: 

  • 1 lb. chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • ½ tsp Turmeric
  • ½ tsp crushed dry mint
  • 2 tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp ginger+garlic paste
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • ¼ tsp chili paste

Rice: 

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Shahi Jeera(black cumin)
  • 6-10 bay leaves, 8 anise stars, 8 pepper corn, 8 cloves, 4 whole green cardamom pods. These can be put in a cloth bag

Method:

Marinade: Mix all the marinade ingredients together in a big bowl, add the chicken pieces to it, mix well and marinate in the fridge for at least 2 hours (or overnight).

Rice: Soak 2 cups rice with some water, salt and shahi jeera for an 1 minute. Drain rice and keep aside.

Preheat the solar oven/grill

Final Preparation:
Layer two dry solar pans each with the following:

  • 1 cup soaked basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee/olive oil
  • 3-5 bay leaves, 4 pepper corn, 4 cloves, 2 whole green cardamom pods and a few anise stars
  • Add marinaded chicken
  • Add 3 tablespoons of fried onion
  • Add 1 cup water, 1 tbsp of olive oil and a pinch of saffron.
      • Cover each pan with parchment paper+steam tray to form a seal. Add a one or two very tiny slits/holes to the parchment paper to help with venting of steam.

      Add the two pans to pre-heated grill and leave for 1 HOUR 15 MINUTES exactly.

      After the time is done, close the solar oven panels. Check the rice and and you can choose to let the pans stay in the oven for another 15 minutes.

      Chicken Biryani is ready to be served.
      Garnish with chopped fresh cilantro and chopped fresh mint before serving.
