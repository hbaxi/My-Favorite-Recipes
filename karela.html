Karela Chutney 

Ingredients:

  • Karela's i.e. Bittergourd - 2 Nos 

  • Green Chillies - 6 - 10 Nos (You can add the chillies as per your taste) 

  • Onion – chopped 

  • Curd - 1/2 cup 

  • Curry leaves – few 

  • Tamarind paste + molasses 

  • Salt 

  • Oil 

 

Method:

  1. Wash and deseed the Karela and chop it very fine.  

  2. Add salt to it and remove the water.  

  3. Heat Oil in Pan add 1/2 tsp mustard seeds when they splutter add the curry leaves  

  4. Add onion and till golden brown.  

  5. Make a paste of tamarind paste, green chillies and molasses.  

  6. Add this paste to the fried onion, mix well and fry for a few minutes.  

  7. Add curd to this.. add salt if required and mix well. Do not make it very watery. Continue to cook for a few minutes 

  8. You may have this with Chapati and Rice as a side dish  


Karela Sabzi 

Ingredients:

  • deseeded & chopped karela - 2 cups 

  • finely chopped onions- 2 cups 

  • grated coconut- 2 cups 

  • chopped coriander leaves- 1 cup 

  • red chilly powder- 1 tbsp.,  

  • coriander powder- 1 tsp.,  

  • 1 tsp garlic paste 

  • 1 tsp.jeera powder 

  • Half tsp.garam masala,  

  • Half lemon juice extract  

  • 1 tsp.turmeric(haldi) powder 

  • 1 tbsp.sugar 

  • 8 tbsp.oil(4+4)  

  • salt to taste.


Method:

  1. Deseed the karela(bittergourd)and cut them into moderately small pieces. Smear salt and turmeric and keep aside for half an hour. Tightly squeeze as much liquid as possible from the karela pieces and discard the juice.

  2. With 4 tbsp.of oil, shallow fry the karela pieces to a light brown and take them out.

  3. Mix these karela pieces along with grated coconut, chopped onions, chopped coriander leaves, garlic paste, masala powders, sugar, lemon juice and salt.  

  4. To the remaining oil in the pan, add the balance of 4 tbsp.oil. When the oil is hot, add the mixture and altogether fry them over moderate heat until the mixture turns dark brown. The Karela subji is ready to serve.

     

Masaledar Karela 

Ingredients:

  • 3 karela (bitter gourd) 

  • 2 green chillies 

  • 1/2 tsp. ginger grated 

  • 1/2 tomato chopped 

  • 4-5 tamarind pieces inch long 

  • 1 stalk curry leaves 

  • 2-3 sprigs coriander chopped 

  • 1/2 tsp. each mustard & cumin seeds 

  • 1 tbsp. molasses or sugar 

  • 1 1/2 tsp. red chilli powder 

  • 1 tsp. dhania powder 

  • 1/4 tsp. turmeric powder 

  • 1/4 tsp. garam masala 

  • 2-3 pinches asafoetida 

  • salt to taste 

  • 1 tbsp. Oil 

  • sour buttermilk to soak karela 

 

Method:

  1. Slice bitter gourd into thin discs.  

  2. Soak in sour buttermilk for 4-5 hours.  

  3. Drain, wash, sprinkle liberally with salt. 

  4. Keep aside for 1 hour. Rub slices well with hand, wash and drain.  

  5. Boil in water till soft to touch, drain, wash again, drain, keep aside. 

  6. Heat oil in a heavy pan, add seeds, asafoetida, allow to splutter.  

  7. Add curry leaves, ginger, tomato, chillies, tamarinds pieces. Stir. 

  8. Mix all dry masalas in 1/2 cup water to form a paste, add to pan.  

  9. Cook till oil separates, add karela slices. Stir, add molasses. 

  10. Stir and simmer for 3-4 minutes. Garnish with chopped coriander.  

  11. Serve hot or cold with chappatis.
