Rice Dishes

 

Lemon Rice 

Ingredients:

  • 1 Lemon (Big in size) 

  • 1/4 tbsp Turmeric Powder 

  • 5 Green Chilies 

  • 1/4 inch Ginger 

  • 1 tbsp Salt 

  • 1 ½ cup Rice. 

 

Seasoning:

  • 1/2 tbsp Mustard 

  • 1 tbsp Chana Daal 

  • a pinch of asafetida 

  • Oil for seasoning. 

 

Method:

  1. Extract the juice from the lemon.  

  2. Add the turmeric powder and the salt to the juice.  

  3. Cut the chilies and the ginger into small pieces.  

  4. Add oil in pan. Oil should be added in excess.  

  5. Once the oil is heated add mustard, chana daal and asafetida.  

  6. As the chana daal turns golden brown, add the lemon juice.  

  7. Allow to boil for 3-4 minutes and let it cool.  

  8. Boil the rice with less water so that the grains are separable from each other.  

  9. Add the prepared lemon syrup to the rice evenly and serve.  

      

Lime Rice 

Ingredients:

  •     2 cups boiled and cooled rice

  •     2 green chillies

  •     1 stalk curry leaves

  •     1 onions finely chopped

  •     1 tbsp. grated fresh coconut

  •     1/2 tbsp. coriander finely chopped

  •     1/4 tsp. each cumin seed, mustard seeds, urad dal

  •     1/4 tsp. turmeric powder

  •     2-3 pinches asafoetida

  •     salt to taste

  •     juice of 1 lime

  •     1 tbsp. oil

     

Method:

       1. Use a heavy or nonstick pan.

       2. Heat oil, add seeds, and dal. Allow to splutter.

       3. Add green chillies, curry leaves, onions, asafoetida

       4. Cook till onions are light pink.

       5. Add rice, turmeric, salt, lime juice, coriander, coconut.

       6. Stir gently till well mixed.

       7. Cover and simmer till steaming hot.

       8. Serve hot.

 

Eggplant Rice 

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 tiny Eggplants
  • 3-4 cloves of Garlic
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 Tbsp. Chana Daal
  • 2 Tsp. Urad Daal
  • 1 ½  Tbsp. Coriander Seeds
  • 2-3 Red Chillies
  • ½ Tsp. Saunf (Anise Seed)
  • ½ inch piece of Cinnamon
  • Salt to Taste
  • Oil
  • 2 cups Rice


Method:

Cook the Rice. Let it cool. Cut the eggplants into small pieces. Chop the onion. Dry roast Chana Daal, Urad Daal, Coriander seeds until the daals turn a little yellow. Add the Red Chillies. Fry a little and then grind a little coarsely. Keep aside. Heat 3 tbsp. of oil. Put Saunf and Cinnamon. Add onions and fry for some time. Now add the garlic and fry. Add the cut eggplants, turmeric, salt and cook until the eggplants are done. Add this to the rice. Add the ground daals to the rice and mix nicely. You could add a little oil if the rice is too dry. Serve with chips or pappad.




Basmati Rice(Microwave) - Regular 


Method:

For 2 cups of regular Basmati Rice, add 3 cups of cold water. Add 1 tsp of ghee and 1 tsp of Shahi Jeera. Microwave uncovered on high for 15 minutes. Continue to microwave for 5 minutes with dish covered. Take dish out of microwave and let stand covered for 5 minutes. Fluff rice with a fork and serve.




Basmati Rice(Microwave) - Brown 


Method:

Combine 1 cup brown rice, 3 cups water. Microwave UNCOVERED on HIGH for 10 minutes. Microwave uncovered 20 minutes on power setting 7 or 8. Allow rice to sit for 5 minutes. Add salt and ghee, fluff with a fork and serve. Do not add salt or butter/margarine to the rice until AFTER the rice is cooked.
